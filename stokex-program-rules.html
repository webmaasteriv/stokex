Stokex Program Rules

Last revised: 25 June, 2021

Experience a new way of earning crypto without trading by inviting your friends to join the Binance community. Invite friends and earn crypto together!

Binance Referral Program Rules:

Spot (Margin) & Futures:

For every new user successfully referred to Binance, the referrer will receive commission on fees anytime the new referred user trades on any of Binance’s Spot (margin) or Futures markets. This means you can now freely invite users via either a Spot referral link or Futures referral link and receive the commission from both. You can also give your friends a discount on trading fees when they sign up using your referral link. (Invite now)

Example A:

User A invites User B via a Spot referral link. User A now receives a referral commission on User B’s trading fees whenever they trade on any of Binance’s Spot, Margin or Futures markets.
*To earn both Spot and Futures commission when referring a friend, you will need to ensure you have first opened a Futures account. If you refer a friend using your Spot referral link, but you have not yet opened a Futures account, then you will only earn commission from their Spot trading fees.

Example B:

User A invites User B via a Futures referral link. User A now receives a referral commission on User B’s trading fees anytime they trade on any of Binance’s Spot, Margin or Futures markets.

Spot and Futures referral programs consolidation:

Binance has linked the trading fee commission for both the Spot and Futures referral programs on 2020-05-11 4:00 PM (UTC). This means you can now freely invite users via either a Spot referral link or Futures referral link and receive the commission from both. However, the linking of commissions does not apply to users referred to Binance via the Spot or Futures referral program prior to 2020-05-07 1:00 PM (UTC).

Visit here to see detailed rules for Spot and Futures referral program updates.

Binance Pool Referral Program:

Binance Pool is a service platform dedicated to improving the income of miners. When your friends create their accounts and start mining on Binance Pool having signed up using your referral link or code, you'll get a percentage of their pool fee income as a referral reward. In addition, you can also get transaction fee referral rewards for Spot, Margin, and Futures.

You can earn 30% commission when you invite your friends to Binance Pool. This means that you will receive 30% of the invitee’s Mining Pool fee income. The final commission rate is calculated by the total estimated profit x mining fee (2.5%) x commission rate (30%).

Visit here to see detailed referral rules for Binance Pool.

Example A:

Our invitee has an estimated profit of 0.0000697BTC. The amount of commission that you would receive would be calculated based on the following:

Estimated Profit x Mining Fee x Commission Rate = Referral Commission

0.0000697 BTC x 2.5% x 30% = 0.00000052 BTC
